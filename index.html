Commonly asked questions about our helping process

How do I schedule a home visit?

Scheduling a home visit is easy. You can either call our appointment line or use our online booking system to choose a convenient date and time. Our team will confirm the appointment and send a healthcare professional to your home.

What types of medical services do you provide at home?

We offer a wide range of medical services, including routine check-ups, treatment for acute and chronic conditions, medication management, wound care, pediatric care, and specialized services. Our goal is to provide comprehensive healthcare in the comfort of your home.

Is it possible to request a home visit for my child?

Absolutely. We have a team of healthcare providers who specialize in pediatric care. They are experienced in addressing the unique medical needs of children and ensuring they receive the best care possible.

How long should I expect to wait for a home visit appointment?

We strive to schedule appointments as soon as possible to meet your needs. Typically, you can expect to have a home visit within a few days, but in the case of urgent requests, we aim to provide care as quickly as possible.

What safety measures do you take during home visits?

Your safety is our top priority. Our healthcare professionals follow strict safety protocols, including wearing protective gear and maintaining the highest hygiene standards. We take every precaution to minimize the risk of infections.
