Are your pet food ingredients natural and safe?

Our pet food ingredients are carefully selected to ensure they are natural, safe, and beneficial for your pet's health and well-being.

Are NutriFur products suitable for all pets?

Yes, NutriFur products are formulated to meet the nutritional needs of a variety of pets, including cats, dogs, birds, and small animals.

What is the shelf life of NutriFur products?

The shelf life of NutriFur products varies depending on the specific item and its packaging. Please refer to the product label for detailed information on shelf life and storage recommendations.

How can I know if my pet has allergies to NutriFur products?

If you suspect your pet may have allergies to NutriFur products, it's best to consult with a veterinarian for proper diagnosis and guidance. Symptoms of allergies in pets may include itching, redness, swelling, or gastrointestinal upset.
