4. Deployment of Cookies

Vacuum Haven uses "Cookies", efficient technologies, and services provided by others to customize your online experience, to display relevant advertising, and to save your preferences on your computer system.

A cookie is a piece of data that is saved on a visitor’s computer by a website and is provided by the visitor’s browser each time they visit the site. Cookies assist us in identifying our visitors, tracking their usage of our website, and understanding their website preference settings. Visitors who wish to not accept cookies should adjust their browsers prior to using Vacuum Haven’s websites. Please be informed that disabling cookies may affect the site’s functioning.

By continuing to use our site without altering your cookie settings, you consent to Vacuum Haven's use of cookies.
