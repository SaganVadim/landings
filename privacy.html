Your Privacy, Your Preferences

LeafLogic respects your preferences by utilizing cookies to enhance your browsing experience and offer personalized recommendations based on your interests. Cookies, small text files stored on your device, enable us to tailor our services to your needs and preferences.

By continuing to utilize our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with LeafLogic's Cookie Policy. Cookies play a pivotal role in enhancing our website's functionality and performance, ensuring a more tailored and user-friendly experience.

We deploy session cookies and persistent cookies on our website to cater to different needs. Session cookies are temporary, expiring upon closing your browser, whereas persistent cookies remain on your device for an extended period. Additionally, we may utilize third-party cookies to analyze website traffic, monitor performance, and deliver targeted advertisements.

You have the autonomy to manage your cookie preferences and opt out of specific types of cookies through your browser settings. However, please be aware that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our website and restrict your access to certain features. By utilizing our website and consenting to cookie usage, you agree to our approach as outlined in our Cookie Policy.

Ensuring Children's Privacy, Upholding Responsibility

LeafLogic upholds the privacy of children by refraining from knowingly collecting personal information from individuals under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we inadvertently collect such information, we take immediate steps to delete it from our records.

Committed to complying with applicable laws and regulations governing children's online privacy, we encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities. If you believe we've collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

Our website isn't intended for use by individuals under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under that age. If you're under 13, please refrain from providing any personal information on our website or utilizing our services without parental consent.

Should you have any questions or concerns about our practices concerning children's privacy, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with all applicable laws and regulations in this regard.
