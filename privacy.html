Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of ExploreAdvisors. At ExploreAdvisors, we prioritize your privacy above all else. This document outlines how we handle your data while you use our website and services. We are committed to securely and respectfully protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We gather a variety of information to ensure your privacy and enhance our customers' experiences:

  • Booking Details: Information regarding your travel bookings, including history, preferences, and booking frequency.
  • Communications: Correspondence exchanged between you and ExploreAdvisors, such as emails, chats, and feedback submitted through the website.
  • Personal Data: Information like name, address, phone number, and email used for processing orders, providing personalized services, and keeping you informed about your travel plans.
  • Navigation: Details about your website usage, such as pages visited and time spent, to improve our service and tailor our offerings to your preferences.
  • Payment Information: Data necessary for processing payments securely and preventing fraudulent transactions.
  • Demographic Information: Information about your demographics and interests to better understand our customer base and improve our services.
  • Social Media Data: Information collected from your interactions with our social media pages, such as likes, comments, and shares, to engage with you effectively.

Data Protection

We have implemented stringent measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access or misuse:

  • Encryption: We utilize advanced encryption technologies to protect your data during transmission and storage.
  • Secure Storage: Your information is stored on secure servers with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular audits to assess and enhance our data protection measures in accordance with industry standards.
  • Employee Training: Our employees undergo comprehensive training on data privacy and security practices to ensure compliance and mitigate risks.
  • Incident Response Plan: We have established procedures to promptly respond to data breaches and minimize impact on affected individuals.
  • Data Retention Policy: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

While no method is completely foolproof, we maintain rigorous security protocols to safeguard your data against potential threats.

Your Rights and Choices

You have important rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to access and obtain a copy of your personal information held by ExploreAdvisors.
  • Rectification: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or if you withdraw consent and there are no overriding legitimate grounds for processing.
  • Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and transmit it to another controller.
  • Restriction and Objection: You can request restrictions on the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, or object to the processing based on legitimate interests, direct marketing, or profiling.
  • Withdraw Consent: If we rely on your consent for processing your personal data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
