Privacy Policy

Welcome to LeafyLuxury Garden Care Services' Privacy Policy. At LeafyLuxury, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect your information when you use our website and services.

How We Ensure Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data, including encryption, secure storage facilities, and regular security audits.

Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information:

When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Consent
