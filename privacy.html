4. Usage of Cookies

PetLand utilizes "Cookies", efficient technologies, and services offered by others to display relevant advertising, personalize your online experience, and remember your preferences on your computer device.

A cookie is a piece of data stored on a visitor’s computer by a website and is provided by the visitor’s browser every time they return to the site. Cookies aid us in identifying our visitors, tracking their usage of our website, and discerning their website preference settings. Visitors who do not wish to accept cookies should adjust their browser settings prior to using PetLand’s sites. Note that disabling cookies may influence the functioning of our site.

By continuing to use our site without changing your cookie settings, you consent to PetLand's use of cookies.
