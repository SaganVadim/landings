Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Protection of Minors' Privacy

LightPainter is committed to protecting the privacy of minors. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you are under 18, please do not attempt to register for our services or provide any personal information about yourself to us. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a minor without parental consent, we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When determining the appropriate retention period, we take into account the nature and sensitivity of the personal information, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of your personal information, the purposes for which we process your personal information, and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means.

International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you are located. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those of your country. When we transfer your personal information to other countries, we will protect that information as described in this Privacy Policy and in compliance with applicable laws.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

LightPainter reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Processing Personal Data

LightPainter processes personal data in accordance with the relevant data protection laws and regulations. We collect and process personal data for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing and maintaining our services.
  • Managing user accounts.
  • Sending administrative information.
  • Personalizing user experience.
  • Analyzing and improving our services.
  • Communicating with users.

We process personal data based on consent, legitimate interests, contractual necessity, legal obligations, or to protect vital interests. We do not process personal data for purposes incompatible with the purposes for which the data was collected.

Sharing Personal Data

LightPainter may share personal data with third-party service providers, affiliates, or other trusted parties for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing services on our behalf.
  • Performing service-related activities.
  • Facilitating communication.
  • Conducting analysis and research.
  • Enforcing our policies and terms.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

We ensure that third-party service providers adhere to privacy and security standards and only share personal data necessary for the provision of services. We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

Security Measures

LightPainter implements technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We employ encryption, access controls, firewalls, and other security protocols to safeguard personal data.

Our security measures are regularly reviewed and updated to address emerging threats and vulnerabilities. We provide training and guidance to our staff to ensure compliance with security policies and procedures.

Despite our efforts to secure personal data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies and terms of third-party services before interacting with them. LightPainter does not endorse or assume responsibility for any third-party services linked to our platform.

Cookie Policy

LightPainter uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and deliver personalized content. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our services. We use cookies for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Authentication and security.
  • Session management.
  • Personalization of content.
  • Analytics and performance monitoring.
  • Advertising and marketing.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings or by using cookie consent management tools. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our services.

Privacy Rights

LightPainter respects your privacy rights and provides mechanisms to exercise those rights. Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You have the right to request access to your personal data.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to restrict processing: You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal data at any time.

To exercise your privacy rights or if you have any questions about your rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy.
