Data Security

At ShapeUp, we prioritize the security of your data. We implement robust measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our security measures include encryption, firewalls, secure socket layer technology (SSL), and regular security audits to maintain the integrity of your data.

Rest assured, we are dedicated to preserving the confidentiality and integrity of your information throughout your fitness journey with ShapeUp.

Data Retention

ShapeUp retains your personal information only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is mandated or permitted by law.

You have the right to request the deletion of your data from our systems at any time, subject to any legal obligations or legitimate business interests that necessitate its retention.
