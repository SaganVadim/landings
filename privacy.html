Our Unwavering Commitment to Protect Your Privacy

Safeguarding the Privacy of Future Chefs

Here at ChefsTable, we understand and highly value the privacy of young, aspiring chefs. We take meticulous care not to knowingly accumulate any personal information from individuals under the age of 18 years. If you are under the age specified, we kindly ask you to refrain from providing any personal details. In the event we come across information collected from a minor without valid consent from their parents, we pledge to delete such information from our servers promptly.

Storage, Retention, and Protection of Your Information

We establish, maintain, and adroitly manage your personal data only for necessary durations to fulfill the purposes explained in our Privacy Commitment, and as necessitated by the law. The duration for which we retain the data is carefully considered - the nature, sensitivity, and the extent of potential risk from unauthorized use or disclosure of the data inform this duration. When considering the legal requirements, the purposes we're processing your information for, and the possibility of delivering these objectives through other means, your information's protection is our priority at every step. We are committed to ensure the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

International Transfers and Processing of Information

In an increasingly globalized world, the information you provide may be processed and stored in a different jurisdiction to your location, which might have different privacy regulations. ChefsTable conforms rigorously to this Privacy Commitment and to applicable laws when your data is transferred across borders. We implement robust safeguards to ensure your personal details remain secure irrespective of which jurisdiction they fall in.

Staying Updated: Changes to Our Privacy Commitment

At ChefsTable, we understand that data protection norms are dynamic and may evolve over time. Accordingly, we may review and revise this Privacy Commitment periodically to accommodate these changes. We will notify about such changes by posting the updated Privacy Commitment on our website, enabling you to stay informed. Your continued use of our services conforms your acceptation of these changes in our Privacy Commitment.

Keeping Lines of Communication Open

If you need answers, have questions or require any further clarification regarding our Privacy Commitment, we urge you to reach out to us. We are always ready to help our patrons with any queries or concerns about their information.

Email: chefstable@gmail.com

Responsible Management of Personal Information

Here at ChefsTable, we are steadfastly committed to the ethical and lawful processing of your personal information. We adhere strictly to relevant data protection laws while collecting and processing your information. The reasons for this collection and processing are numerous, including providing and honing our services, managing user accounts, dispatching administrative updates, customizing user experiences, constructing insightful analytics, and maintaining effective communication with you. We ensure that personal data processing is scrupulous and carried out as per regulatory provisions and only for legitimate and necessary purposes.

Transparent and Constraints on the Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share your personal data with third-party service providers, affiliates, or trusted partners to facilitate the provision of our services, including dispatching class schedules, undertaking payment processing, data analysis, communication, and providing user support. We are judicious about who we partner with, basing these relationships on the other entities' commitment to uphold and value your privacy. We furnish them with only as much information as would be necessary to deliver their services to you. Selling your personal data or renting it out for profits is strictly against our ethos.

Ensuring Security and Integrity of Data

ChefsTable employs rigorous technical and operational measures to safeguard your data against any unauthorized or illicit use, access or processing. We utilize advanced encryption techniques, controlled and limited access, and comprehensive security protocols for this purpose. In an ever-evolving digital landscape with emerging threats, we regularly revisit and revise our security measures to ensure the safety of your data. Our commitment is towards securing your personal details, while acknowledging that no digital platform can provide one hundred percent security.

Navigating the World Wide Web: External Links

Our platform may house links to third-party websites, products, or services that do not come under our purview. We strongly recommend our users to go through the respective privacy policies of these third-party sites before navigating them further. ChefsTable neither endorses these third-party sites nor are we liable for their privacy practices, their content, or their policies.

Exercising Your Privacy Rights

As a responsible culinary school, ChefsTable recognizes and respects your rights in relation to your personal data. Depending on your geographic location and local privacy laws, these rights may include the right to request access, rectification, erasure, and restriction of processing of your personal data, and request portability of your data. Our team is available to answer any questions about these rights, aid you to exercise these rights, and to provide more information about how we ensure that these rights are respected.
