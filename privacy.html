Cookies Policy

AxisEstates uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized recommendations based on your interests. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website, enabling the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with the AxisEstates Cookies Policy. Cookies help us improve the functionality and performance of our website, allowing us to deliver a more personalized and user-friendly experience.

We use both session cookies and persistent cookies on our website. Session cookies are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device for a longer period. We may also use third-party cookies to analyze website traffic, monitor website performance, and deliver targeted advertisements.

You can manage your cookie preferences and opt-out of receiving certain types of cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and limit your access to certain features. By using our website and consenting to the use of cookies, you agree to our use of cookies as described in our Cookies Policy.
