Privacy Policy

Welcome to ManiPediMagic, your go-to destination for expert manicure and pedicure services. We highly value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data, as well as how we handle and share your information in relation to our services.

Information We Collect

At ManiPediMagic, we respect your privacy and only collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it. This may occur when you reach out to us through our website, email, or other communication channels. The information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to share. Unlike many websites, we do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to gather data about your browsing habits or online activities.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide enables us to deliver and improve our nail care services, respond to your inquiries, and manage our communications with you. For instance, we may use your contact details to send important updates, service notifications, or respond to your questions. Additionally, if you have opted in, we may send you newsletters, special offers, or other marketing content to keep you informed about the latest from ManiPediMagic. You are free to opt-out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link included in the emails or by reaching out to us at ManiPediMagic@gmail.com.

How We Share Your Information

Your privacy is important to us, and we do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services. These may include companies that handle payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting, or customer support. Each of these providers is required to keep your information confidential and use it solely for the purpose of providing services to ManiPediMagic. We may also disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our company or others.

Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no security system is completely foolproof. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Any information you transmit to us is done at your own risk, and we encourage you to take precautions when sharing sensitive information online.

Your Choices

You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at ManiPediMagic@gmail.com, and we will assist you promptly. Additionally, if you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, you can opt-out by using the unsubscribe link provided in any of our emails or by contacting us directly.
