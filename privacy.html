4. Usage of Cookies

CoolFlow uses "Cookies", proficient technologies, and services offered by others to display relevant advertisements, personalize your online experience, and recollect your preferences on your computer device.

A cookie is a data item stored on a visitor's computer by a website and is provided by the visitor's browser every time they return to the website. Cookies assist us in recognizing our visitors, tracking their usage of our website, and determining their website preference settings. Visitors who do not wish to accept cookies should configure their browser settings before using CoolFlow’s websites. Note that disabling cookies may influence the functioning of our site.

By continuing to use our site without adjusting your cookie settings, you consent to CoolFlow's use of cookies.
