TasteTrek Culinary Academy implements stringent security measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We continuously assess and update our security protocols to ensure the highest level of protection for your data.

Children's Privacy

TasteTrek Culinary Academy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
