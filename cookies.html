Types of Cookies We Use

We deploy both session and persistent cookies, as delineated below:

  • Session cookies: These ephemeral cookies remain in your device's memory while browsing our site, but vanish when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These linger on your device for a set duration, even after closing your browser, enabling us to recognize you on your subsequent visit.
