How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to maximize your cuisine exploration on our website:

  • To streamline your online experience by recalling your preferences.
  • To collect anonymized data about visitor interactions, assisting us to optimize our website and resources.
  • To shape our course offerings based on your food tastes and cooking interests.

Types of Cookies We Use

We make use of both session and persistent cookies, as outlined here:

  • Session cookies: These transitory cookies are held on your device while browsing our website and vanish once your browser is closed.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device for a specified duration, enabling us to identify your device during your future visits.

Your Cookie Preferences

It's your right to accept or reject cookies. While most browsers typically accept cookies as a matter of course, you have the discretion to modify your settings to repudiate them. Please be advised that altering such settings could impact the functionality of the site and your user experience.
