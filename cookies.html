Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 12-06-2023

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our HomeRevive website. Their primary purpose is to enhance website performance and improve the user experience, as well as collect information about site usage for the benefit of site administrators.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your home improvement browsing experience by remembering your selected settings.
  • To compile anonymous data about user behavior for analytical purposes.
  • To enable specific home improvement features.

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize both session and persistent cookies, which are defined below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies remain in your device's memory while you explore our home improvement site and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a defined period, even after you close your browser, helping to recognize you as a returning user.

Your Cookie Preferences

Users have the right to either accept or decline cookies. While most browsers initially accept cookies, you can customize these settings to decline cookies if that is your preference. Please note that such adjustments may impact the website's performance and overall home improvement experience.
