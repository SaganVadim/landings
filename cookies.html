Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize both session and persistent cookies, which are defined below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies remain in your device's memory while you explore our website and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a defined period, even after you close your browser, helping to recognize you as a returning user.
