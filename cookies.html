What We Offer

At LensCraft, we provide a creative space for photography and capturing special moments. Our primary aim is to enhance the photography experience, collect data for photographic improvement, and support photographers on their journey.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your photography browsing experience by remembering your selected settings.
  • To compile anonymous data about user behavior for analytical purposes.
  • To enable specific photography features.

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize both session and persistent cookies, which are defined below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies remain in your device's memory while you explore our photography site and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a defined period, even after you close your browser, helping to recognize you as a returning user.

Your Cookie Preferences

Users have the right to either accept or decline cookies. While most browsers initially accept cookies, you can customize these settings to decline cookies if that is your preference. Please note that such adjustments may impact the website's performance and overall photography experience.
