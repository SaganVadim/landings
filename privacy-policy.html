Commitment to Your Privacy at BarkBrigade

Securing Trust with Our Pet Parents

At BarkBrigade, we understand how important your privacy is. We’re not just dedicated to training your pets—we’re equally committed to safeguarding your personal information. Every detail you share with us is treated with the highest level of care and respect. We recognize the trust you place in us, and we work hard to earn and maintain it by ensuring your data is safe and used responsibly. Whether you're a seasoned pet owner or just beginning your journey with a new furry friend, your privacy matters to us. Our policies are designed to protect not only the personal information of our adult clients but also to safeguard any data related to minors. We are vigilant in collecting data only with proper consent and are quick to delete any unauthorized data we might inadvertently collect.

Privacy isn’t just an afterthought at BarkBrigade—it's at the heart of how we do business. We don’t just train pets; we build lasting relationships based on trust. That’s why we strictly limit data processing to only what’s necessary for improving your experience with us. Our team members are trained to handle personal information with care, ensuring that we only process data after receiving your explicit permission. We never use your information for commercial purposes unless you request it directly, and we are transparent about how we collect and use your data. Your trust is our priority, and we work diligently to keep it intact.

Protecting Your Information

Your personal information is valuable, and at BarkBrigade, we go the extra mile to ensure it's fully protected. Just as we take every step to train and care for your pets, we approach the protection of your data with the same level of diligence. Our systems are built to store your data securely, retaining it only for as long as necessary to provide you with exceptional services. This includes a careful evaluation of the sensitivity of the data, the potential risks of misuse, and compliance with legal retention requirements. We continuously update our security protocols to protect your data from any unauthorized access or breaches.

We use advanced encryption techniques for data transfers, meaning that whenever you share information with us, it's fully encrypted and protected. Access to your data is tightly controlled within our team, ensuring that only authorized personnel handle it, and even then, only as necessary. Additionally, all of our employees undergo regular security training to stay updated on the best practices in data protection. We also continuously monitor and enhance our technological defenses to stay one step ahead of any potential threats. At BarkBrigade, your data's safety is as important to us as the well-being of your pets.

Global Privacy Standards

BarkBrigade is proud to serve pet owners from around the world, which means that in some cases, your personal information might be processed in different countries. However, no matter where your data is handled, we guarantee that it is treated with the same level of security and care that we uphold in every part of our business. We strictly follow global privacy regulations, such as the GDPR, to ensure that your information is protected under the highest standards, regardless of its geographical location.

In addition to our in-house privacy protocols, we work only with trusted third-party service providers who share our commitment to protecting your data. These partners are thoroughly vetted to ensure they meet our stringent privacy requirements. We ensure that your personal data is safeguarded at all stages of processing, and we monitor these third-party interactions closely to prevent any mishandling of information. BarkBrigade goes above and beyond to ensure your data is treated with respect and dignity, no matter where in the world we’re helping pet owners train and care for their companions.
