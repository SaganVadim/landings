Privacy Policy - Yardz

Welcome to Yardz's Privacy Policy. We take your privacy very seriously and are fully committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains in detail how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when visiting our website or purchasing products from our store. By continuing to use our site, you agree to the terms set out in this policy, and we assure you that we will handle your information with the utmost care and responsibility.

Please note, this Privacy Policy applies only to the information collected directly through our website. It does not cover external websites or third-party services that may be linked from our pages. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of these external sites before sharing any personal information with them. Staying informed about how your data is handled across different platforms is essential to maintaining your privacy.

Information We Collect

At Yardz, we collect only the necessary information you choose to provide. This may include your name, email address, shipping information, and payment details when you make a purchase or subscribe to our newsletter. We may also gather non-personal information, such as your browsing behavior on our site, to help us understand how we can improve your experience. Rest assured, we do not collect any sensitive information without your explicit consent.

In addition, we may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences and help you navigate the site more efficiently. However, you have the option to disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to use them. We always strive to respect your choices and provide transparency regarding the data we collect.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to process your orders, provide customer support, and keep you informed about new products, promotions, or updates related to Yardz. We may also use your data to improve our website functionality, ensuring that your experience is as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Occasionally, we may send out marketing communications, but only with your permission.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. The only exception to this rule is when sharing information with trusted partners, such as payment processors or shipping companies, to complete transactions. These partners are required to adhere to strict confidentiality agreements and use your data solely for the purposes of completing your order.

How We Secure Your Information

Keeping your information safe is a top priority at Yardz. We employ industry-standard security measures, including data encryption, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. Our team regularly reviews our security practices to ensure they are up-to-date with the latest technologies and threats.

While we make every effort to protect your information, it is important to understand that no method of online data transmission or storage is completely secure. As a result, we cannot guarantee absolute security. However, we are continuously improving our security protocols to ensure that your personal data is as safe as possible.

Links to External Websites

For your convenience, our website may contain links to external websites or services, including social media platforms, affiliate sites, or third-party service providers. Although we carefully select these links, Yardz is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external websites. Each site may have its own privacy policy, and we recommend reviewing those policies before interacting with or providing any personal information to them.

Remember, once you leave our website and access an external link, your interactions are governed by the privacy policies of that site. We encourage you to be vigilant and make sure you understand how your information is being collected and used on any site you visit.

Children’s Privacy

Yardz is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our records.

We strongly encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities to ensure their safety. If you believe that your child has provided personal information to Yardz without your consent, please contact us immediately, and we will take the necessary actions to resolve the issue.
