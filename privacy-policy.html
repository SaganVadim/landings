Your Privacy Matters at Nailicious

We Care About Your Data

At Nailicious, we believe that your personal information is just that—personal. Whether you're booking your next manicure or leaving feedback, we handle your details with the respect they deserve. We’re committed to making sure that your privacy is protected at all times. We don’t just follow the rules—we go above and beyond to ensure that your experience is safe and secure.

We also take extra precautions when it comes to minors. If you're under 18, we ask that you refrain from sharing any personal information with us. And if we accidentally collect such data, rest assured that it will be immediately deleted. Your safety and privacy are our priorities.

How We Protect Your Data

The personal info you share with Nailicious isn’t just numbers in a database—it’s part of the trust you place in us. To protect that trust, we make sure your information is securely stored and only kept for as long as needed to provide our services, or as long as the law requires. We assess how sensitive your data is and take the necessary precautions to ensure it’s safe from misuse or unauthorized access.

Here's how we safeguard your data:

  • State-of-the-art encryption and security technologies to ensure your information stays confidential
  • Strict internal policies on who can access your information, ensuring that only authorized staff can handle it
  • Ongoing training for our team, so they're always up-to-date on the latest security best practices

International Data, Same Protection

At Nailicious, we’re a global brand, and sometimes your information may be processed in locations outside of your home country. But wherever your data goes, our commitment to protecting it stays the same. We adhere to strict international privacy standards, making sure that your information is protected no matter where it’s processed. From Europe to Asia, we treat your data with the same care and protection.

We take special steps to ensure that any data shared internationally is handled in line with our privacy policy and local regulations. Rest assured, your data’s journey is as secure as your next trip to the salon!

Keeping You in the Loop

We know how quickly things change, especially in the digital world. That's why we keep our privacy practices up-to-date with the latest legal standards and technological advancements. Whenever there’s a significant change to how we handle your information, we’ll let you know via this page. You can always check back here to stay informed about how we’re protecting your data.

By continuing to use our services after we’ve updated our privacy policy, you’re agreeing to the new terms. But don’t worry—we’ll make sure to communicate any major changes clearly so that you’re always in the know.

We Respect Your Data

Nailicious isn’t just here to make your nails look amazing—we’re also here to make sure your personal data is used ethically and responsibly. We only collect information that helps us improve our services and give you the best possible experience. Whether it’s managing your appointments, sending you special offers, or personalizing your next visit, we use your data with care.

Importantly, we’ll only process your personal data with your consent, and you always have the right to choose how much you share with us. If you ever change your mind or want to limit the information you provide, just let us know—we’re happy to accommodate.

Sharing Data Responsibly

At Nailicious, we sometimes work with trusted third-party providers to help deliver the best service to you. Whether it’s payment processing, marketing, or customer support, we make sure that any partner we work with follows our strict privacy guidelines. We don’t sell or rent your personal data to anyone—your information is safe with us.

  • Only the most reliable third-party providers, chosen for their expertise and trustworthiness
  • Strict agreements to ensure they comply with our privacy policy and protect your data
  • No selling or renting of personal data—ever

Your Data’s Safety is Key

Your personal data is like a hidden gem, and we treat it that way—guarding it closely with the latest cybersecurity measures. We continuously monitor our systems to ensure everything is secure, and we’re always on the lookout for potential risks. If there’s ever a hint of a problem, we act fast to resolve it, keeping your data safe and sound.

  • Ongoing monitoring of security measures to prevent breaches
  • Regular updates to our cybersecurity systems
  • Swift action in case of any detected threats to your privacy

Your Rights Matter

At Nailicious, you’re in control. Whether you want to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, we’re here to help. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights related to your data, and we’re happy to assist you in exercising them.

Don’t hesitate to get in touch with us if you have any questions about your data rights or need assistance—our team is here to make sure your privacy is respected and protected at every step of the way.
