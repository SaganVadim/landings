Your Privacy is Our Story at NovelNook

Protecting the Secrets of Young Readers

At NovelNook, we cherish the privacy of our young book enthusiasts. We are devoted to ensuring that no personal information is gathered from individuals under the age of 18. If you are under this age, please avoid sharing any personal details with us. Should we inadvertently collect information from someone underage without proper parental consent, we will swiftly remove it from our records.

Preserving and Safeguarding Your Data

We handle your personal data with the utmost care, retaining it only for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy and as required by law. The retention period is carefully assessed, taking into account the nature and sensitivity of the data, and the risk of unauthorized use or disclosure. Our priority is to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information throughout its lifecycle. We are committed to protecting your data with the highest standards.

Global Reach: Handling Your Information Across Borders

In our interconnected world, your information may be processed and stored in locations different from where you are. Different jurisdictions may have varied privacy laws. At NovelNook, we adhere strictly to our Privacy Policy and relevant laws, ensuring that your data is protected, regardless of its location. We implement robust measures to secure your information across all borders.

Staying Current: Updates to Our Privacy Policy

As privacy standards evolve, so does our Privacy Policy. We review and update our practices periodically to stay in line with current regulations and practices. We will inform you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website. Continued use of our services implies your acceptance of these updates.
