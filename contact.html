FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about TripSage. Our FAQ section is here to assist you in making informed decisions.

What age range are your tours suitable for?

Our packages cater to a wide range of ages, from children to seniors. We have something for every traveler, regardless of their age or interests. Whether you're looking for a family-friendly vacation or a solo adventure, we have the perfect package for you.

Do you offer travel insurance services?

Yes, we offer complimentary travel insurance services for all bookings made with TripSage. Make your trip extra secure with our inclusive insurance options. At TripSage, we understand the importance of insuring your travel investment. That's why we've partnered with industry-leading insurance providers to offer you the best coverage options.

Are your tours safe for travelers?

Safety is our top priority at TripSage. We rigorously evaluate all our tours to ensure they meet the highest safety standards for travelers of all ages. As part of our commitment to safety, we work with local tour operators who are experienced, knowledgeable and licensed in the countries we operate in.

Do you offer international travel packages?

Yes, we offer international travel packages to various countries. Contact us for more information on destinations and travel options. We specialize in creating bespoke travel experiences that cater to your unique preferences and travel goals. From cultural immersion to adventure, we have the perfect package for you.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, we accept cancellations and reschedules within the allowed timeframe. Contact us for assistance with modifications to your booking. Note that any changes made to your booking may be subject to additional fees.
