Cookie Policy

Enforcement date: 12-06-2023

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our Makeup Marvel site. Their primary function is to enhance the performance of websites and improve user experience, as well as gather information about site usage for the benefit of site administrators.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your browsing experience by remembering your chosen settings.
  • To compile anonymous data about user behavior for analytical reasons.
  • To enable specific social media features.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session and persistent cookies, which are defined below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies remain in your device's memory while you explore our site and are automatically purged once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a defined period, persisting even after you close your browser. They assist in recognizing you as a returning user.

Your Cookie Preferences

Users have the right to either accept or decline cookies. While most browsers initially accept cookies, you can tailor these settings to decline cookies if that is your preference. Be aware that this adjustment might affect the website's performance and overall user experience.
